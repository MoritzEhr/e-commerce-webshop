About Us

OnlyFans was founded with a clear and simple purpose: To provide high-quality ventilation products without the noise — both literally and figuratively.

In 2020, we noticed a gap in the market. While general electronics retailers carried fans, they were often treated as an afterthought: limited selection, questionable build quality, and little product expertise. That’s when we decided to create a focused, reliable alternative.

At OnlyFans, we specialize — and we believe there’s strength in that. By concentrating solely on fans, we’re able to offer a carefully curated range of products, backed by industry knowledge and real customer support.

We serve both residential and commercial clients who value comfort, efficiency, and long-term reliability. Whether it’s a whisper-quiet desk fan for your home office or a high-performance ceiling fan for larger spaces, we make it easier to find the right fit — and deliver it fast.

Our team combines product engineers, customer service professionals, and logistics experts, all aligned around one goal:
Better air, made simple.

We don’t just sell fans. We stand behind them!

Mission & Values
At OnlyFans, we believe comfort should be effortless.

Our mission is to make climate control simple, effective, and accessible — without compromising on quality, design, or support. We focus entirely on fans, because we believe in doing one thing exceptionally well.

Our values guide every decision we make:

  • Reliability – We stock only tested, high-performance products built to last.
  • Clarity – No confusing jargon, no upselling — just honest guidance.
  • Customer-first thinking – We listen, we support, and we follow through.
  • Sustainability – We prioritize energy-efficient models and responsible logistics.

Our goal isn’t just to sell fans. It’s to be the most trusted name in residential and commercial ventilation.
What Makes Us Different
We’re not a generic tech warehouse. We don’t sell phones, blenders, or microwaves.

We sell fans. That’s it — and that focus makes all the difference.

While larger retailers offer broad selection with minimal product knowledge, we do the opposite. At OnlyFans, we offer a refined range of fan models chosen for performance, durability, and design — and we actually know how they work.

Here’s what sets us apart:
  • Narrow focus, deep knowledge – Every product is vetted by our team.
  • Real support – Questions are answered by specialists, not scripts.
  • No drop-shipping – Our inventory is stored, handled, and shipped directly by us.
  • Transparent information – Accurate specs, honest comparisons, and clear use cases.

This isn’t a side category for us. This is the business.
Behind the Scenes
What you see in the store is the result of a lot that happens behind the scenes.

Our selection process starts with technical benchmarks — airflow performance, decibel levels, energy use, build quality — and ends with hands-on testing in real-world conditions. If it doesn’t meet our standards, we don’t sell it.

We also take pride in fast, careful fulfillment. Orders are packed at our own facility to reduce damage, delays, and surprises. And when something goes wrong, we don’t hide behind ticket systems or long response times — we solve it.

We invest in doing things properly — from supplier partnerships to packaging design — because details matter.
