Frequently Asked Questions

Which type of fan is right for me?
That depends on the room size and how you plan to use it. For small spaces, table or pedestal fans are ideal. For larger rooms, tower or ceiling fans work better. If you're looking for targeted cooling, choose a model with oscillation and multiple speed settings.
What’s the difference between a tower fan and a pedestal fan?
Tower fans are space-saving, quieter and usually feature a modern design with extras like remote control or timers. Pedestal fans are generally more powerful, height-adjustable, and can be positioned more flexibly in a room.
How loud are your fans?
Noise levels vary by model and speed setting. Our quietest models operate below 40 dB – about the sound level of a whisper. For bedrooms, we recommend models with a "silent" or "sleep" mode.
Do your fans come with remote controls or smart features?
Many of our models include remote controls, timers, and even app-based controls via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth. You can check each product’s specifications for available features.
How do I clean my fan?
Always unplug the fan first. Wipe the outer surfaces with a soft, damp cloth. For pedestal or table fans with protective grills, you can gently remove the cover to clean the blades. Regular cleaning improves both hygiene and performance.
How energy-efficient are your fans?
Our fans are much more energy-efficient than air conditioners. Most models use between 30–60 watts per hour. Some also include energy-saving modes for even lower consumption.
Do your fans come with a warranty?
Yes, all our fans include a standard 2-year warranty. Some models offer extended manufacturer warranties. Check the product details or contact our support team for more information.
What should I do if my fan is defective?
Please reach out to our customer service. We’ll assist you promptly, whether it's a repair, exchange or return. Having your invoice or order number ready will speed up the process.
Do you offer replacement parts?
Yes, we stock common replacement parts such as fan blades, grills, remote controls, and bases. Just send us a message with your model number and we’ll help you find what you need.
Can I use my fan in the winter too?
Absolutely. Some fans have a reverse function that helps circulate warm air from heaters more efficiently. This can actually reduce heating costs in colder months.
